Add a neighbourhood battery that can charge/discharge to reduce peak load.

Minimum features:

Battery has:
Capacity (kWh)
Max charge/discharge power (kW)
Round-trip efficiency (optional)
A control strategy that aims to reduce peaks:
e.g., keep neighbourhood load below a threshold, or discharge during top N% load periods
Visualization that demonstrates impact:
Show “net neighbourhood load” with and without battery, or show battery power and state-of-charge.
Highlight peak shaving effect (e.g., max load reduced, peak periods flattened).
